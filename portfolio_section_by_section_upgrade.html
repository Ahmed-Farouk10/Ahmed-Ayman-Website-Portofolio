Navbar — "AAF" logo + anchor links

Currently

Plain sticky nav, text logo "AAF", 6 anchor links with no animation. Functional but invisible — exactly what you expect from a template.

Must upgrade

NavigationMenu

shadcn/ui

Replace your raw anchor links with this accessible, animated dropdown-ready nav. Use as your structural base, then style with Tailwind.

shadcn add navigation-menu

Sheet

shadcn/ui

Slide-in mobile menu drawer that replaces hamburger menus. Slides from the right with a smooth overlay. Replaces your current mobile nav completely.

shadcn add sheet

AnimatedShinyText

Magic UI

Wrap your "AAF" logo in this — a subtle shimmer animation runs across the text continuously. Turns a plain monogram into a living brand mark.

npx magicui-cli add animated-shiny-text

Magnetic nav links (DIY with GSAP)

GSAP

On hover, each nav link subtly moves toward the cursor. 20 lines of GSAP. One of the most noticed details on top-tier sites — juanmora.co does this exactly.

gsap.to(link, { x: magnetX, y: magnetY })

Scroll progress bar

Framer Motion

A thin line at the top of the navbar that fills as the user scrolls. useScroll() + useSpring() from Framer Motion. 5 lines of code, huge polish signal.

const { scrollYProgress } = useScroll()
